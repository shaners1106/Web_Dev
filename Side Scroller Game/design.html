Our side scroller game is the result of assimilating multiple visions into one fluid design. We were inspired by childhood memories of playing games like Super Mario Bros, Terarria and Google dinosaur games. While the technology behind modern video games has left games such as these in the dust, revisiting the nostalgia of childhood when times were simpler is a goal that we hope you will experience playing our game. So many of us have great memories of simple games like this, running, jumping, learning how to dodge imminent enemies. We hope you enjoy playing our game and on some level catch a glimpse into the past.
Created by Ryan Grady, Sakina Vidyarthi and Shane Snediker.
